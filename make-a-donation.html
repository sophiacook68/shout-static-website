Why We Need Your Support

A fully funded campaign makes a real difference

SHOUT is run by a small committee of concerned resident home-owners. No one on the SHOUT Committee will gain financially from the work undertaken — our time and energy is given free of charge.

But to run a fully effective lobbying and social media campaign, we need financial contributions from our supporters. Every donation, however large or small, goes directly towards:

Professional lobbying

Engaging expert advisers to help us navigate Parliament and make the most effective case to Ministers and MPs.

Leafleting and print

Reaching homeowners who may not be aware of the HVCTS and its potential impact on them and their communities.

Digital outreach

Running effective social media and digital advertising campaigns to build public awareness and political pressure.

Website and research

Maintaining this site and commissioning or publishing independent research and legal analysis to support our case.

Donate online via PayPal

Secure, quick, and no PayPal account required. Every contribution goes directly to the campaign.

Donate now

SHOUT is an independent campaign. Donations are not tax-deductible. No one on the Committee benefits financially.

Other Ways to Help

Can't donate? You can still make a difference

SHOUT also needs support from those who could be affected by a tax on homes. You can help if:

  • You could be affected, now or in the future
  • You're worried about friends or family who could be adversely affected
  • You're concerned about the potential impact on your community or neighbourhood
  • You object in principle to an annual cash levy on a non-cash generating asset
  • You would like to be involved in the campaign in any way

Please write to your MP expressing your concerns, and join our mailing list to receive occasional campaign updates.
